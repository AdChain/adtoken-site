The objective of the Rinkeby Rover mission is to offer a preliminary look at the adChain Registry DApp and to gain valuable insights from the adToken community. The mission will serve as twofold: (1) to educate the adToken community on interacting with the adChain Registry and (2) to gather feedback to guide us towards Ethereum Colonization.

The Lift-Off phase marks the deployment of the adChain Registry DApp to the Rinkeby Testnet.

The Discovery phase is when the adToken community can explore and experiment with the adChain Registry DApp.

The Voyage Home is when we will close the adChain Registry DApp on the Rinkeby Testnet and marks the beginning of the Refueling Stage.

The Refueling Stage is when the adChain Core Dev team will take all of the gathered intel from the Rinkeby Rover and make mission-critical updates to the adChain Registry smart contracts in preparation for Ethereum Colonization.
The objective of Ethereum Colonization is to permanently deploy the adChain Registry smart contracts onto the Ethereum Mainnet and re-open the adChain Registry DApp to adToken holders.

The Lift-Off phase marks the deployment of the adChain Registry DApp to the Ethereum Mainnet.

The Lunar Colony is the phase when the entire adChain ecosystem is permanently deployed onto the Ethereum Mainnet, resulting in a fully opened and decentralized adChain Registry DApp.
