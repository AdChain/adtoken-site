CONTRACT FUNCTION TO EXECUTE
purchaseTokens()

The purchaseTokens() smart contract function needs to be executed in order to purchase tokens. You must send ETH when calling purchaseToken() function to buy adToken. Further instructions are detailed below.

IMPORTANT NOTES

  • Do not send ETH directly to the adToken sale contract address. Please follow instructions further down on how to properly execute the purchaseTokens() smart contract function in order to purchase adToken.
  • Do not send funds from exchanges such as Poloniex, Bitfinex, Bittrex, Coinbase, GDAX, etc.
  • Do not send ether before the specified start block time. You can view the lastest block number here.
  • We recommend using the wallet applications mentioned below.
  • Only ETH is accepted. Do not try sending anything other than ETH.
  • Always double check addresses you are sending ETH to.
  • By participating in the token sale, you agree to the terms of use and purchase agreement.

MyEtherWallet Mist Parity MetaMask
MyEtherWallet

MyEtherWallet is a popular and reliable application that can be used to generate accounts and send ether to the adToken sale contract.

If you do not have an account with MyEtherWallet and you'd like to create one, please start on step 1.

If you already have an ethereum account address and ether in the account, please skip to step 7.

GENERATING WALLET

  1. Visit myetherwallet.com.

  2. Select the tab "Generate Wallet", insert a strong password, and click on the "Generate Wallet" button.

  3. Click on the "Download" button to download the Keystore File. Store this file in a secure location. This will give you access to your account. Click on "I understand. Continue." button.

  4. Print your paper wallet and store in a secure location. Never share your Private Key with anyone. Click on "Next: Save your Address" button.

  5. Select your keystore wallet file and enter your wallet password. Click on "Unlock" button.

  6. Scroll down and you will see your "Account Address". You will have to send ether to this account address in order to fund your wallet required for purchasing adToken.

ACCESS ADTOKEN SALE CONTRACT

  1. Click "Contracts" navigation item.

  2. Enter the adToken Sale Contract address found above in the "Contract Address" field.

  3. Enter the adToken Sale Contract ABI JSON found above in the "ABI / JSON Interface" field.

  4. Click on "Access" button.

  5. In the "Read / Write Contract" dropdown, select "purchaseTokens". Then restore your wallet.

  6. Click “Write” button and a dialog window will appear prompting for Amount to Send. Enter the desired amount of ETH you'd like to use to purchase adToken. Set the Gas Limit to 100,000.

  7. Verify that the inputs are correct and click on "Yes, I am sure! Make transaction." button.

  8. You will see at the bottom of the screen a message saying that the transaction was sent successfully. You can click on the "View your transaction" link to view the current state.

  9. Here you can see etherscan website displays that the transaction was successful.

Mist

If this is your first token purchase, we recommend that you complete it using MyEtherWallet. If you have experience using Mist, please follow the instructions shown below:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of Mist installed.

  2. Open the Mist application.

  3. Go to the "Contracts" tab

  4. Click on the "WATCH CONTRACT" button.

  5. In the "Watch contract" dialog window, enter the following:

    • In the "CONTRACT NAME" field, enter "AdToken Sale".

    • In the "CONTRACT ADDRESS" field, enter adToken Contract Sale Address found above.

    • In the "JSON INTERFACE" field, enter adToken Contract Sale ABI JSON found above.

  6. Click the "OK" button.

  7. Click on the "AdToken Sale" contract.

  8. Scroll down. You should see a form title "WRITE TO CONTRACT".

    Fill in the form with the following information:

    • In the "Select function" dropdown, choose "purchaseTokens".

    • In the "Execute from" dropdown, choose your ethereum account.

    • In the "Send ETHER" field, enter the desired amount of ETH you'd like to use to purchase adToken.

  9. Click on the "Execute" button.

  10. In the Execute contract dialog, input 100,000 as the Gas limit.

  11. Enter the account password and click on "SEND TRANSACTION".

  12. You can check the status of the purchase in etherscan website.

Parity

If this is your first token purchase, we recommend that you complete it using MyEtherWallet. If you have experience using Parity, please follow the instructions shown below:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of Parity installed.

  2. Start Parity application.
    Note: if using testnet you have start parity first by running "parity --chain=kovan" in command line.

  3. Navigate to http://127.0.0.1:8180 in your browser.

  4. Enable the Contracts tab by checking the checkbox where it say "Contracts" under the "Settings" page.

  5. Go to "CONTRACTS" tab and click on "+ WATCH" menu item.

  6. Select "Custom Contract" option and click the "Next" button.

  7. In the "ENTER CONTRACT DETAILS" form, enter the following:

    • In "network address" field, enter adToken Contract Sale Address found above.

    • In "contract name" field, enter "AdToken Sale".

    • In "contract abi" field, enter adToken Contract Sale ABI JSON found above.

    Then click on "+ ADD CONTRACT" button.

  8. You will now see the newly added contract under "CONTRACTS". Click on "AdToken Sale" contract.

  9. Click on "EXECUTE" button.

  10. In the "FUNCTION DETAILS" form, set "function to execute" dropdown to "purchaseTokens()"

  11. In the "transaction value (in ETH)" field, enter the desired of ETH you'd like to use to purchase adToken.

  12. Click on "POST TRANSACTION" button.

  13. Enter Account Password to unlock account and click on "CONFIRM REQUEST" button.

  14. You can check the status of the purchase in etherscan website.

MetaMask

MetaMask is a simple browser extension that allows you to send tokens easily and securely. If this is your first token purchase, we recommend that you complete it using MyEtherWallet. If it's not your first time then please follow the instructions below to purchase adToken using MetaMask.

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of MetaMask installed.

  2. Click on the MetaMask extension/add-on icon in your browser.

  3. Click on the "SEND" button.

  4. Copy the adToken contract address found above and paste it in the "Recipient Address" field.

  5. In the "Amount" field, enter the amount of ETH you would like to spend on purchasing adToken.

  6. Enter "0x3290ce29" in the "TRANSACTION DATA (OPTIONAL)" field.

  7. Click on the "SEND" button.

  8. Enter 100,000 in the Gas limit field.

  9. Click on the "ACCEPT" button.

  10. Transaction will appear under "HISTORY" section.
