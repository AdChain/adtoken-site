MetaMask

MetaMask is a simple browser extension that allows you to send tokens easily and securely. If this is your first token purchase, we recommend that you complete it using MyEtherWallet. If it's not your first time then please follow the instructions below to purchase adToken using MetaMask.

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of MetaMask installed.

  2. Click on the MetaMask extension/add-on icon in your browser.

  3. Click on the "SEND" button.

  4. Copy the adToken contract address found above and paste it in the "Recipient Address" field.

  5. In the "Amount" field, enter the amount of ETH you would like to spend on purchasing adToken.

  6. Enter "0x3290ce29" in the "TRANSACTION DATA (OPTIONAL)" field.

  7. Click on the "SEND" button.

  8. Enter 100,000 in the Gas limit field.

  9. Click on the "ACCEPT" button.

  10. Transaction will appear under "HISTORY" section.
