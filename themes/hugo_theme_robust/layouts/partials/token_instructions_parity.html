Parity

Parity is a secure browser-based wallet that enables you to schedule future purchases. Please follow the instructions below to purchase adToken through Parity:

  1. Open your Parity app.
  2. Open the “Accounts” tab and select the account you would like to transfer funds from.
  3. Click “Transfer”
  4. Paste the adToken contract address found above in the “recipient address” field.
  5. Select the ETH amount.
  6. Click “Send”. You will need to confirm by entering your password.
