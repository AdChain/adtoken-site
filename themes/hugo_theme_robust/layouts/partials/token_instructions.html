MetaMask

MetaMask is a simple add-on that allows you to send tokens easily and securely. Please follow the instructions below to purchase adToken using MetaMask.

  1. Click on the extension/add-on icon in your browser.
  2. Click “Buy”.
  3. Copy the adToken contract address found above and paste it in the “Recipient Address” field.
  4. Enter the amount of ETH you want to send.
  5. Click “Send”.
