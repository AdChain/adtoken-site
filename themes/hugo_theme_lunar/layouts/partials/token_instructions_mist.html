Mist

If this is your first token purchase, we recommend that you complete it using MyEtherWallet. If you have experience using Mist, please follow the instructions shown below:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of Mist installed.

  2. Open the Mist application.

  3. Go to the "Contracts" tab

  4. Click on the "WATCH CONTRACT" button.

  5. In the "Watch contract" dialog window, enter the following:

    • In the "CONTRACT NAME" field, enter "AdToken Sale".

    • In the "CONTRACT ADDRESS" field, enter adToken Contract Sale Address found above.

    • In the "JSON INTERFACE" field, enter adToken Contract Sale ABI JSON found above.

  6. Click the "OK" button.

  7. Click on the "AdToken Sale" contract.

  8. Scroll down. You should see a form title "WRITE TO CONTRACT".

    Fill in the form with the following information:

    • In the "Select function" dropdown, choose "purchaseTokens".

    • In the "Execute from" dropdown, choose your ethereum account.

    • In the "Send ETHER" field, enter the desired amount of ETH you'd like to use to purchase adToken.

  9. Click on the "Execute" button.

  10. In the Execute contract dialog, input 100,000 as the Gas limit.

  11. Enter the account password and click on "SEND TRANSACTION".

  12. You can check the status of the purchase in etherscan website.
