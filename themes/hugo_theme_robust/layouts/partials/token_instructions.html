MyEtherWallet

MyEtherWallet is a popular and reliable service that can be used to send ETH to our token sale. If you do not have an account with MyEtherWallet and you’d like to get one, please start on step one. If you already have an account with MyEtherWallet, please skip to step 5. Please follow the steps below in order to use it correctly:

GENERATING WALLET

  1. Visit https://www.myetherwallet.com.
  2. Select the tab “Generate Wallet”, insert a strong password, and click on the “GENERATE” button.
  3. MyEtherWallet gives you two options on how to store your private key. The first option is download the .JSON file with an encrypted version of the private key and requires a password to decrypt. The second option is to download an unencrypted version of the .JSON file.
  4. Save your wallet address or QR code. In order to receive transactions, you must provide the senders with a public key to which they can send the ETH to.

PURCHASING ADTOKEN

  1. Select the tab “Send Ether & Tokens”.
  2. Select the option for which to open your ETH wallet in order to login to your account.
  3. Copy the ETH wallet address found above. Please make sure that the address used is from the official adToken site. Insert the amount of ETH to send and finish by clicking “Generate Transaction”.
Mist

If this is your first token purchase, we recommend that you complete it using MyEtherWallet. If you have experience using Mist, please follow the instructions shown below:

  1. Open your Mist app.
  2. Go to the “Send” tab in the options.
  3. Paste the adToken ETH address in the “To:” section. Make sure you copy the contract address from above.
  4. The fee should be set to the faster end of the bar. Click “Send”.
  5. A summary of the transaction will appear. Please note, the transaction has not yet been generated.
  6. Enter your password again and click “Send Transaction”.
Parity

Parity is a secure browser-based wallet that enables you to schedule future purchases. Please follow the instructions below to purchase adToken through Parity:

  1. Open your Parity app.
  2. Open the “Accounts” tab and select the account you would like to transfer funds from.
  3. Click “Transfer”
  4. Paste the adToken ETH address found above in the “recipient address” field.
  5. Select the ETH amount.
  6. Click “Send”. You will need to confirm by entering your password.
imToken

If you would like to purchase adToken on your mobile device, you now can with imToken. Please follow the instructions outlined below:

  1. Open the imToken app on your mobile device.
  2. Select the “Discover” option on the bottom menu.
  3. Click on the “ICO” option.
  4. Find the “AdToken” icon and click on it.
  5. At the bottom of the screen, you will have the option to enter an amount of ETH.
  6. Click “Join Now” and enter your password.
  7. Click “Confirm”.

MetaMask

MetaMask is a simple add-on that allows you to send tokens easily and securely. Please follow the instructions below to purchase adToken using MetaMask.

  1. Click on the extension/add-on icon in your browser.
  2. Click “Buy”.
  3. Copy the ETH wallet address found above and paste it in the “Recipient Address” field. Please make sure that the address used is from the official adToken site.
  4. Enter the amount of ETH you want to send.
  5. Click “Send”.
